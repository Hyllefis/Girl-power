# Girl Power — Training Journal

## Deploy to Vercel (5 minutes)

1. Go to https://vercel.com and sign up for free
2. Click "Add New Project"
3. Click "Import" and choose "Upload" 
4. Upload this zip file
5. Click Deploy — done! ✓

## Add to your phone home screen

**iPhone (Safari):**
1. Open your Vercel URL in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow)
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add"

**Android (Chrome):**
1. Open your Vercel URL in Chrome
2. Tap the 3 dots menu
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add"

The app will appear on your home screen like a native app called "Girl Power" 🌸
